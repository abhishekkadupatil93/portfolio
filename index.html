About Me

I am a full stack software professional with over 6+ years of experience in web application development primarily within the BFSI domain. I am currently working as a Consultant at Mindcraft Softwares Pvt. Ltd., where I am assigned the role of Team Lead for a client engagement with SBI Life Insurance.

I began my career as a Java trainee at Clover Infotech Pvt. Ltd., where I was assigned to Bajaj Finserv. After a year, I was promoted to Software Engineer and continued my association with Clover for nearly four years. During this period, I spent over three years working with Bajaj Finserv and BHFL and later transitioned to SBI Life Insurance for a new engagement. Following this, I moved to Mindcraft Softwares Pvt. Ltd. on the recommendation of my client, where I was retained as a Consultant with team lead responsibilities. I have been associated with SBI Life Insurance for the past three years through Mindcraft.

Throughout my career, I have worked across the complete software development lifecycle, starting from requirement understanding and design to development, deployment and production support. My technical journey began with Java and expanded to include JavaScript, jQuery, and Oracle WebCenter Sites as a CMS platform, along with exposure to Linux environments, application servers and system architecture. I have also been actively involved in stakeholder communication across technical and business teams.

From a leadership perspective, I have handled key ownership roles at client sites. At Bajaj Finserv, I worked as a Vertical Lead for the B2B module, leading a team of five members. Later, at SBI Life Insurance, I was entrusted with the role of Team Lead for a team of ten members. My responsibilities included technical decision-making, delivery ownership, mentoring team members and regular coordination with senior stakeholders including Managers, AVPs, VPs and SVPs. Based on my performance and ownership, I was retained by SBI Life Insurance through Mindcraft.

Technically, I have a strong command over Oracle WebCenter Sites, including solution design, frontend UX integration and Spring-based backend development. I also possess a good understanding of enterprise server architecture, databases, WebLogic servers and Solr-based applications. I focus on building stable, maintainable, and scalable solutions aligned with business objectives.

In addition to technical delivery, I bring a strong client and business orientation. I have worked closely with product owners, business analysts, managers and senior leadership. I was also part of a critical SBI Life Insurance initiative that contributed to the organization achieving a Guinness World Record. Across all my engagements, I have consistently demonstrated ownership and accountability for the solutions I deliver.

At this stage of my career, I am looking to transition into higher leadership roles such as Assistant Manager or Manager, where I can take on broader responsibilities, lead larger teams and contribute to challenging initiatives that offer long-term growth and impact.
